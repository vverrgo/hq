Alex Amaya

About

Alex Amaya, born and raised in Louisville, Kentucky, is a talented actor with a robust background in both theatre and film. He earned his Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in Acting from The Theatre School at DePaul University, where he also received certifications with recommendation in Unarmed, Single Sword, and Quarterstaff combat from the Society of American Fight Directors (SAFD). During his time at The Theatre School, Alex's credits included playing Jason in Sweat (Dir. Aurelia Clunie), Birdman in Burn (Dir. Nicole Ricciardi), Howie in Do You Feel Anger? (Dir. Rebecca Willingham), and Smartnoggin in Squirrel Girl Goes to College (Dir. Michelle Lopez-Rios).

Ben Sztainberg), and Perfectly Sane (Dir. Olivia Salib & Leila Boisvert).

Further enriching his acting skills, Alex has trained with Kentucky Shakespeare and Prague Shakespeare. Based in Chicago, IL, he is passionate about making rap music and sampling unusual sounds, blending his creative pursuits in performance and music.
